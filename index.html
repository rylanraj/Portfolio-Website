Bouncy Ball Game (Java Swing)

  • Project Description: Used the Timer class to simulate frames via an ActionListener to make a ball drawn onto the JPanel fall by increasing its y coordinates exponentially to simulate gravity. The player could tap the space bar resulting in the ball bouncing back up by temporarily making gravity negative. Pairs of obstacles moving horizontally at varying heights can be passed resulting in a point for the player. If the player has the ball collide with an obstacle, it results in a game over.
